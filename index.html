GREATMINDS CLUB OF ABUJA

Great minds Club Abuja Nigeria is registered with CAC as a social club working to better the lives of it's members through synergies and strategic programs/activities that is geared towards improving their social lives, welfare, business and cooperate existence. The Club strives to instill discipline and brotherly fraternity of love towards each other and also provides some CSR to better our society and environment. Established over 17 years ago;with a cooperative and sports team as existing structures.

Greatminds sports committee

The Greatminds Spprts Committee is an appendage of Greatminds Club formed with a view to keeping members physically and emotionally fit , stable at all times, and also to foster bonding, friendship among members and friends In fulfillment of its mandate, the Sports committee created a football League competition among members and friends. The football league competition was formed in 2021, and it is already in its 3rd year. The 2023 of the Football League is currently ongoing

Apearl Premier Private School

Apearl Premier Private School is a co-educational, Circular, and Inclusive school that provides facilities and resources that are world-class. Here, maximum care and support that ensure children attain optimum performance for greatness in society are given. In Apearl teaching and learning are ICT driven. The Curriculum is rich; the British Curriculum is infused into the Nigerian Curriculum to ensure cross-curricular linkages that are project and inquiry-based, focusing on promoting lifelong learning and survival skills for the 21st century. Our Vission: Nurture and Empower the Child for Excellence. Our Mission: To enable children to achieve their true potential in a friendly and safe environment.
